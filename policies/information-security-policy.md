## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose
This policy establishes the framework for protecting [Organization Name]'s information assets against unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction. It defines the principles, responsibilities, and minimum security requirements for all personnel.

### 1.2 Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees (permanent, temporary, contractors)
- Third-party vendors with access to organizational systems
- All information assets regardless of storage medium or location
- All systems, networks, and infrastructure owned or managed by [Organization Name]

---

## 2. Policy Statement

[Organization Name] is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of its information assets. Security is a shared responsibility across all levels of the organization.

---

## 3. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| **Board of Directors** | Ultimate accountability for information security governance |
| **CISO / Security Lead** | Strategy, policy development, risk management, incident oversight |
| **IT Management** | Technical implementation, system hardening, vulnerability management |
| **Department Heads** | Enforcing policy within their departments, risk identification |
| **All Employees** | Compliance with security policies, reporting incidents, protecting credentials |
| **HR Department** | Security awareness training, exit procedures, background checks |

---

## 4. Information Classification

All information must be classified according to the following schema:

| Level | Description | Handling Requirements |
|-------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Public** | Information intended for public release | Standard handling, no restrictions |
| **Internal** | Organization-internal information | Share only within the organization |
| **Confidential** | Sensitive business information | Need-to-know basis, encryption required |
| **Restricted** | Highly sensitive data | Encryption required, access logged, approval needed |

---

## 5. Access Control

### 5.1 Principles
- **Least Privilege:** Users receive minimum access necessary for their role
- **Need-to-Know:** Access granted based on business requirements
- **Segregation of Duties:** Critical functions split among multiple individuals

### 5.2 Requirements
- All user accounts must be uniquely identifiable (no shared accounts)
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required for all systems
- Access reviews conducted quarterly
- Immediate revocation upon termination or role change

---

## 6. Acceptable Use

### 6.1 Authorized Use
Organizational resources may only be used for:
- Authorized business activities
- Incidental personal use that does not interfere with work or incur costs

### 6.2 Prohibited Activities
- Unauthorized access to systems or data
- Installing unapproved software
- Bypassing security controls
- Using organizational resources for illegal activities
- Sharing credentials or passwords
- Unauthorized data exfiltration

---

## 7. Incident Response

All security incidents must be reported within 1 hour of discovery to: security@[organization].com

### Incident Severity Levels

| Level | Definition | Response Time |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| **Critical** | Active data breach, ransomware, system compromise | 15 minutes |
| **High** | Confirmed unauthorized access, malware infection | 1 hour |
| **Medium** | Policy violation, suspicious activity | 4 hours |
| **Low** | Security awareness issue, minor policy breach | 24 hours |

---

## 8. Compliance and Enforcement

### 8.1 Compliance
All personnel must:
- Read and acknowledge this policy annually
- Complete security awareness training
- Report security concerns promptly
- Cooperate with security investigations

### 8.2 Non-Compliance
Violations of this policy may result in:
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination
- Legal action where applicable
- Revocation of system access

---

## 9. Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or upon significant organizational changes. Updates will be communicated to all personnel.
